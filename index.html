Elden Ring Bosses

Elden Ring hosts a great number of bosses, who have varying mechanics and difficulty. This website is a description of some of the bosses.

Margit, the Fell Omen

Margit, the Fell Omen is one of the first bosses you meet in Elden Ring. He is a large, humanoid boss with a sword-like staff. He has a variety of attacks, including a charge, a slam, and a spin attack. Even though he is a relatively easy boss his attacks tend to be misleadingly telegraphed and provoke many beginners to dodge excessively, only to end up caught by surprise and defeated.


Margit's attacks

Attack Description
Charge Margit charges towards the player, dealing heavy damage.
Slam Margit slams his greatsword onto the ground, creating shockwaves.
Spin Attack Margit performs a spinning attack, hitting all nearby enemies.

Margit's drops

  1. Margit's Wooden Staff
  2. Margit's Laurel Crown
  3. Margit's Golden Tooth
